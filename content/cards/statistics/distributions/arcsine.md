## Arcsine Distribution

The PDF is

$$
\frac{1}{\pi\sqrt{x(1-x)}}
$$

for $x\in [0,1]$.

It can also be generalized to

$$
\frac{1}{\pi\sqrt{(x-1)(b-x)}}
$$

for $x\in [a,b]$.
